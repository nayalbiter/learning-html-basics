Pumpkin Cheesescake Recipe

Do you love sweet pumpkin? Enjoy this easy recipe of Pumpkin Cheesecake. The pumpkin crust with amaranth gives it a crunchy touch that is perfect with the creamy cheesecake. The best thing about this Pumpkin Cheesecake is that you don't need an oven to prepare it!

8 Portions

Ingredients

Ingredients for the base:
1 1/2 cups cinnamon cookies
1/4 cups toasted peanuts
1/4 cups pumpkin seeds
1/4 cups amaranth
3/4 cups butter (melted)
Ingredients for the cheesecake:
4 packages cream cheese
1 cup whipping cream
1 1/4 cups icing sugar
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 tablespoon orange zest
2 envelopes unflavored gelatin powder, hydrated and melted
Ingredients for the pumpkin syrup:
3 piloncillos
1 cup of water
3 cups winter squash
1 stick cinnamon
1 star anise pod
2 allspice berries
1 orange peels
Ingredients to decorate:
4 envelopes unflavored gelatin powder, for the pumpkin candy
1 1/2 cups whipped cream
pumpkin seeds

Preparation

  1. Place the cookies in a resealable bag and with a rolling pin, crush the cookies until obtaining a fine powder.
  2. Mix the cookies with the roasted peanuts, pumpkin seeds, amaranth and butter. Place in a ring with waxed paper and press to compact. Set aside.
  3. For the cheesecake, beat the cream cheese until it softens, add the whipping cream, the powdered sugar, the cinnamon and the orange zest. Add the unflavored gelatin slowly and beat until integrated. Pour over crust and refrigerate for 2 hours or until set.
  4. For the pumpkin syrup, in a pot over medium heat cook the piloncillo with water until you have a light caramel, add the pumpkin, cinnamon, star anise, pepper, and the orange peel, cook until the pumpkin falls apart around 30 minutes. Let cool.
  5. Blend the pumpkin syrup and add the unflavored gelatin slowly. Pour over the cheesecake and return to refrigeration until set.
  6. Unmold the cheesecake, decorate with whipped cream and pumpkin seeds.

TIPS:Once you add the unflavored gelatin to the cheesecake, beat vigorously, so that it does not set.

PRESENTATION:On a cake base, accompanied by coffe.
